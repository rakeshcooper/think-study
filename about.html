Our History

ThinkStudy is a unique educational consultancy company. Mr. Sathyanarayana Reddy, a prominent educationist with 22 years of expertise in the field of education, established the company. With the intention of establishing a platform where students can explore their aspirations of studying abroad with useful advice, motivation, and awareness. With over two decades of experience in guiding students in their education and career trajectory, we can assist individuals reaching their full potential.

What we do?

Many individuals find overseas educational standards to be complicated and puzzling. We acknowledge that the decision to study abroad is a profound commitment. That is why we have professionals that are experienced and well-informed about all aspects of international education, including opportunities after completing studies abroad.
